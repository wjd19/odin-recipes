Bill's Typical Breakfast

Ingredients

  • Three eggs
  • Two slices whole wheat toast
  • Butter
  • Preferred sprinkle cheese

Instructions

  1. Crack three eggs into frying pan, turn stove on medium heat
  2. Place two pieces of toast into toaster, place setting for preferred level of darkness
  3. Scramble eggs and sprinkle cheese as desired once eggs start to harden
  4. Once toast is toasted, spread butter
  5. Enjoy!
